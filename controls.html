Welcome to the Controls document.

This is the place where you can learn more about the controls and how the game work on the server.

Controls:

Right click - Kick the ball.

Tip: Facing up to the sky will cause the ball to fly higher.

Left click - Catch the ball. (Available if you're the team's goalie)

Running into the ball will let you dribble it.

Arrows key will changes the direction when you kick the ball.

Dash (-) will decrease the kick speed while Plus (+) or Equal (=) will increase the kick speed.
